At NORVIA, we believe in combining human expertise with technology to shape the future of recruiting.

Instead of a typical first interview, we are using a video interview format. Your video will be reviewed shortly after recording by your NORVIA recruiter.

Meet your Recruiter at NORVIA

A recruiter from NORVIA’s team will review candidate videos in the early hiring stage and decide whether they move on to the next stage.

Trained in NORVIA’s structured hiring guidelines, the recruiter analyzes what you say and how you say it, looking for signs of competence, confidence, and fit with NORVIA’s values.

During your interview, the recruiter will look at key aspects of your answers to understand how you might fit the role at NORVIA.

Watching your interview, the recruiter evaluates:

  • Your answers – how you present your strengths, motivation, and fit for NORVIA
  • Your communication – fluency, tone, and pacing
  • Your behavior – eye contact, expression, and posture
  • Your presence – how confident and authentic you appear

What to Expect

Your interview at NORVIA consists of two parts:

  1. Self-introduction (1 minute)
    Please pitch yourself freely and authentically to your recruiter. This is your opportunity to showcase your background, motivation, and personal strengths.
  2. Spontaneous interview questions (3 × 1 minute)
    You will then be asked three questions from your recruiter. These are designed to get an impression of your personality, including your strengths and weaknesses. Please answer them spontaneously and honestly.

You may complete the interview in German or English, depending on what you feel most comfortable with – our recruiters are fluent in both languages.

  • While you complete the interview, we will record your video and audio. Your recruiter will review your interview shortly afterwards.
